Smart Payment Planning Tips

• Set Up Auto-Pay: Never miss deadlines with automatic payments
• Budget Monthly: Save $250-400/month for property taxes
• Use Escrow: Most mortgages include tax escrow management
• Payment Timing: Make payments several days before due date for processing time
